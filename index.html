The Voice of Kurdish YouTube

Peshawa Barznji is a prominent Kurdish YouTuber from Sulaymaniyah, Kurdistan, known for his engaging gaming content and vlogs. He began his YouTube journey on June 29, 2013, and uploaded his first gaming video, a fan-made Uncharted remix, on July 14, 2016. His channel features a diverse range of gameplay videos, including popular titles like Minecraft, God of War, The Last of Us Part II, Marvel's Spider-Man: Miles Morales, and Red Dead Redemption. Peshawa is recognized for his family-friendly content, where he often translates game dialogues into Kurdish, making them accessible to a broader audience.

Over the years, his channel has amassed over 1 million subscribers and more than 352 million views, reflecting his significant impact in the gaming community. Notably, his "Hello Neighbor" series has garnered over 900,000 views per video. His dedication to producing quality content in the Kurdish language has not only entertained but also fostered a sense of community among Kurdish-speaking gamers worldwide.
